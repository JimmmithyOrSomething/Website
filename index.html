ICE BEAR

The greatest thing on the planet

"The world is not ready for what Ice Bear can do"

-Ice Bear

Ice Bear is one of the three main characters from the animated series We Bare Bears, created by Daniel Chong. Ice Bear is the most stoic and mysterious of the three bears. He is the youngest of the three brothers—Grizzly, Panda, and himself—and is known for his cool and composed demeanor. Unlike Grizzly, who is outgoing and Panda, who is sensitive, Ice Bear is quiet, calm, and prefers to be alone or engage in very specific activities, such as cooking or martial arts.

Personality Traits

Ice Bear is unique compared to his brothers. He often speaks in short, serious sentences and doesn't show much emotion. Despite this, he is a very skilled individual, with abilities ranging from advanced cooking to self-defense tactics. His ice-cold personality may seem intimidating to others, but beneath it, Ice Bear has a kind heart and cares deeply for his brothers.

One of Ice Bear’s most notable traits is his ability to remain calm in almost any situation, whether he's facing danger or simply hanging out with his brothers. This calmness gives him a mysterious aura, making him one of the most interesting characters in the series.

Skills and Hobbies

Ice Bear is highly skilled in various areas. One of his most notable hobbies is cooking. His culinary skills are unmatched, often preparing gourmet meals for his brothers with minimal ingredients. Ice Bear also enjoys martial arts, as he frequently practices self-defense techniques and has demonstrated great proficiency in hand-to-hand combat. He is also known for his silent, almost ninja-like movements when performing tasks or protecting his family.

Notable Moments

  • The Cooking Competition: In this episode, Ice Bear participates in a cooking competition where he showcases his unmatched skills in the kitchen. Despite his quiet nature, Ice Bear’s cooking skills win the judges over.
  • Martial Arts Master: Ice Bear demonstrates his martial arts prowess in multiple episodes, often using his skills to protect his brothers or solve problems without uttering a word.
  • Ice Bear’s Secret Life: Ice Bear often shows a secretive side of his life. Whether it’s his fascination with snow globes or his deep knowledge of animal behavior, Ice Bear has a world of his own that he keeps hidden from others.

Relationships with Other Characters

Despite his serious demeanor, Ice Bear cares deeply for his brothers, Grizzly and Panda. While Grizzly tends to be the energetic and outspoken leader, and Panda is the emotional, tech-savvy brother, Ice Bear serves as the calm, silent protector. He has a unique bond with each of them. With Grizzly, he shares moments of quiet support, and with Panda, he often offers sage advice without saying much.

Ice Bear also has an interesting dynamic with other characters in the show, including his interactions with humans. While he may seem unapproachable to strangers, Ice Bear’s actions often reveal a deep sense of loyalty and love for his family.

Fun Facts

  • Ice Bear has an unusual way of speaking. He often uses third-person phrases, referring to himself as "Ice Bear" instead of "I" or "me".
  • He has a massive collection of snow globes, a hobby that remains one of his most secretive interests.
  • Despite his icy exterior, Ice Bear is surprisingly good with animals and has a deep connection with them.
  • Ice Bear’s cooking is so good that even his toughest critics are amazed by his culinary talents.
